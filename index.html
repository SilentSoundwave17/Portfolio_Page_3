    Jimmy's Feed Co.
    Mural


    A locally-run sandwich store in Waterloo comissioned me to design and paint a mural on a wall inside of their store.

    The owner, Jimmy Boone, asked for a depiction of well-known celebrities, products, and landmarks from the Kitchener, Waterloo and Cambridge regions.

    the
    Process

    I first created a digital prototype of the mural before getting approval to transfer the mural to the wall.

    I then marked off the space, and started sketching the design on the wall in pencil.

    After finishing the pencil sketch, I used acrylic paint markers to paint over the lines and add detail.

    Overall, the mural took 3 months to complete.

    I would like to thank the wonderful staff at Jimmy's Feed Co. for this wonderful opportunity, and for all the delicious sandwiches I ate while working there.
